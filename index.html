Explore Like a Local

Our curated guide to the best spots nearby.

Iconic Delhi

India Gate

A majestic war memorial and a popular spot for evening strolls.

Humayun's Tomb

A stunning example of Mughal architecture that inspired the Taj Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The world's tallest brick minaret, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Chandni Chowk

Dive into the chaotic charm of Old Delhi for unforgettable street food and shopping.

Gaur City, Greater Noida

Experience the modern, well-planned side of NCR with entertainment, shopping, and dining right at your doorstep.

Gaur City Mall

Your one-stop destination for shopping, movies, and a wide variety of restaurants.

City Park

A large, green space perfect for a relaxing walk or a morning jog.

Venice Mall

Famous for its Grand Canal and gondola rides, offering a unique shopping experience.

Local Eateries

Explore the numerous cafes and restaurants in Gaur City for both local and international flavors.
